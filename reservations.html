About Us

Berwick Manor is a premier dining destination offering a unique blend of fine cuisine and a relaxed shisha lounge atmosphere. Our chefs use only the freshest ingredients to create dishes that are both innovative and comforting.

Our lounge provides a sophisticated setting to unwind with a variety of shisha flavors and handcrafted cocktails. Join us for an evening of culinary excellence and refined relaxation.
